You can even trade in your old smartphone for credit.

  • Trade In Trade in your current device for credit towards a new iPhone. To protect your data and privacy, we’ll show you how to safely back up and wipe your data before you trade in your old phone.

  • Support There are lots of ways to reach someone if you have questions. Call a Specialist, chat with someone online or Tweet @AppleSupport. Whether your question is about hardware or software, we’re ready to help. And the Tips app will help you get the most out of your iPhone over time.

  • Move to iOS app Start by downloading the Move to iOS app on your Android phone. It securely transfers the stuff you care about most — your contacts, messages, WhatsApp content, photos, videos, email accounts and calendars — from your Android phone to your iPhone.
